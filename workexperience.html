Work Experience

This is what I do.

Throughout my career path, I've gained extensive hands-on experience across diverse industries, which have significantly enriched my skill set and work ethic. I started my journey at UPS as a seasonal delivery driver, where I refined my logistical expertise and commitment to delivering exceptional service on time. Managing various delivery routes and ensuring precise package deliveries instilled in me a deep sense of accountability and meticulous attention to detail.

Transitioning into the construction sector, I joined a specialized flooring company focused on gym floor installations and property renovations. This role immersed me in the intricate art of flooring installations and exposed me to the dynamic challenges of property enhancement. Working on rental properties, I gained comprehensive knowledge of property maintenance, ranging from basic repairs to comprehensive renovations.

Through my experience, I have acquired a diverse skill set in the construction industry.

My career then led me into the automotive industry, where I worked at a semi-truck autoparts store. Here, I expanded my knowledge of heavy-duty vehicle components and customer service, assisting customers with their truck part needs and refining my technical skills while maintaining a customer-centric approach.

Seeking further growth, I ventured into transportation with a trucking job that involved loading semis onto larger trucks for transport to various locations. This role demanded efficiency, teamwork, and precision in handling large-scale logistics, highlighting my adaptability and ability to thrive in fast-paced environments.

Each position has played a unique role in shaping my professional development, instilling invaluable skills such as problem-solving, adaptability, and a strong work ethic. I look forward to leveraging this diverse background in my future endeavors, approaching every challenge with a proactive and resourceful mindset.
